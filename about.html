Hello, I'm Eleanor Morton-Smith!

Front-end developer based in the UK

I started out my journey in making and constructing costumes for London theatre productions. This is where my love for creating, and building processes along with meticulous problem solving started.
From there, I branched out into other areas of the entertainment industry and found a love for voice acting and audio engineering. When working with numerous directors, fellow actors and audio engineers I found myself evermore drawn to the technical side of audio engineering, curious to discover more about the inner workings of digital audio workspaces.
This lead me to discover my passion for development - it gives me the opportunity to combine my skills in creating and problem solving in a constantly evolving landscape.
