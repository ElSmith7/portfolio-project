Hello, I'm Eleanor Smith!

Front-end developer based in the UK

I'm a front-end developer with a kaleidoscope of experiences that add flair to my skill set. Before delving into coding, I ventured through various industries in search of the perfect fit. I started in London theaters, crafting costumes and enjoying the thrill of solving puzzles. Then I leaped into the entertainment world, lending my voice to video games, podcasts, animation, and more. I've also dabbled in customer service, honing my adaptability and communication skills.

But here's the twist that led me to coding—imagine me, a curious soul, working on a cosplay project—a glove that buzzed with the light of electricity! Teaming up with a friend, we tinkered with circuits, and I got a taste of coding. Who knew this encounter would ignite a passion for development? Back then, societal expectations nudged me toward artsy pursuits, but my heart was already set on tech.

In 2022, destiny played its hand when I stumbled upon SheCodes—an opportunity to learn something new. It was love at first code! Coding blends creativity and intricate problem-solving in a fascinating way. The endless possibilities and constant growth hooked me in. I knew this was the path I wanted to explore—the sky's the limit!

I'm on a quest to leave a mark on the tech world, inspiring positive change, and making it an exciting place for all!
